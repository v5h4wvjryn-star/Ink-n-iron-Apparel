Ink & Iron Apparel LLC

North Richland Hills · Texas

Ink & Iron Apparel is a bold, independent print-on-demand apparel brand born and built in North Richland Hills, Texas. We create premium garments that speak loud without saying a word — gear designed for people who stand for something.

We specialize in patriotic designs, holiday celebrations, and the cultural moments that bring people together. From the Fourth of July to game day, every drop is crafted to honor the grit, grind, and spirit of the people who wear it.

  • Premium blanks — Comfort Colors & Lane Seven
  • Print-on-demand quality, built to last wash after wash
  • Original patriotic & celebration designs
  • Proudly based in North Richland Hills, Texas
